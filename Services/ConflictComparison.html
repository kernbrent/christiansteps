Common Conflict Management Approaches
Approach Core Idea Strengths Limitations
Avoidance Conflict is minimized or delayed Prevents escalation, useful for minor issues Issues remain unresolved; resentment can grow
Accommodation Preserving harmony by yielding Maintains relationships in the short term Personal needs ignored; imbalance forms
Competition Winning the conflict Decisive; useful in emergencies Damages trust; produces losers
Compromise Each side gives something up Practical and fast Best solution often missed
Collaboration Seeking mutual understanding and growth Builds trust; lasting solutions Requires time, humility, maturity
Christian Reconciliation (Our Approach) Truth, grace, repentance, restoration Heals relationships; reflects Christ Requires surrender and spiritual maturity
